2XU - WOMEN'S COMPRESSION SLEEVED TRISUIT - 2020

 

 

The latest version of the 2XU women's Compression Sleeved Trisuit is designed to be an ideal long-course racing suit.

Taking as its starting point the need to minimise the three biggest factors in long-distance racing - injury, fatigue, heat - as its starting point, it features the latest in triathlon innovation, to deliver big on performance, comfort and style.

So key features includes superior muscle stabilisation, heat-beating technology, extra cushioning for a smooth ride, and unrivalled aero-dynamics This version has sleeves, so is for you if you prefer additional coverage when racing.

 

Recommended Activity: TRIATHLON

 

 

Garment Features:

105D POWER MESH PWX COMPRESSION: To stabilise your muscles and give you more power output and less fatigue
QUADRICEPS MAPPING: To support and reduce soreness in your key upper leg muscles
HIGH-FILAMENT, LOW-GAUGE FABRIC WITH SWISS COLDBLACK FINISH: Heat-beating technology to keep you cool in the hottest conditions
PRO LD CHAMOIS: For cushioning on the bike and a low-profile feel on the run
PREMIUM ITALIAN-ENGINEERED SILICONE GRIPPER: For a secure fit - as well as being perforated for comfort
ERGONOMICALLY ENGINEERED PANELLING: Unrivalled comfort and maximum aerodynamics
EASILY ACCESSIBLE REAR POCKETS: For keeping your race-day essentials protected and safe
1/4 LENGTH SEMI-AUTO LOCK ZIP
SLEEVED DESIGN: Great if you like the additional cover

 

Fabric Benefits:

105D POWER MESH PWX COMPRESSION: Premium support for your lower body that supports your muscles and prevents fatigue
SWISS COLDBACK FABRIC: Heat-beating technology to keep you cool
PRO LD CHAMOIS: Includes 80-density low-water absorption foam for added support in the aero position, and soft edging for less bulk on the run. Extremely comfortable and low profile, while still providing the cushioning required for long-distance racing.
SBR VENT: Delivers extreme cooling properties while being exceptionally aerodynamic.

 

 

The Brand: 

2XU (“Two Times You”) is an Australian sportswear brand, launched in 2005, and specialising in compression and recovery-wear.

As the compression partner to some of the world’s best teams, its compression products are a vital part of any athlete’s sports kit: helping you to recover, regenerate and perform at your best.
