The dryrobe Advance long sleeve is designed to let you get active outdoors, whatever the weather.

With a dryrobe, you can be protected from the elements anywhere, and stay warm in any climate. The long-sleeve design provides extra warmth and is ideal when you're waiting to compete or watching sporting events.

dryrobe is not your average fit; it will be large and loose enough to pull your arms inside and get changed. So you can keep doing the things you love outdoors, with the world's most advanced change robe.

About dryrobe:

Founded by British surfer Gideon Bright, who brings to bear lessons gained from more than 30 years' experience on the water, dryrobe was born after he asked his mum to create something to make getting changed outdoors more comfortable.

Her towel-lined cape has been sold commercially since 2010, and is now used by many top outdoor sportspeople. dryrobe supplies GoPro ambassadors and competitors with a range of co-branded robes.
